A Brief History

Hello! Welcome to the Cypress Creek Orchestra website. Along with Florida's own Cypress Creek High School came our orchestra program, teaching the art of music and raising talented string instrumentalists. For the past 20 years, Cypress Creek's orchestra program has been led by our teacher Jimmy Gilliam, who has been responsible for preparing generations of students to create iconic performances.

Now in the 2024-2025 school year, we are at our strongest we have been for many years! Come support our many performances, collaborations with the rest of the performing arts department, and even our individual members as well!

Our main Philharmonic Orchestra plays as a C difficulty level orchestra making the jump to the B difficulty level. We also have our Beginner Orchestra program, which can serve to raise any interested rookies!

Looking to join the Cypress Creek High School Orchestra? Anyone can join! You don't even have to be a student to support our us and our community. Thanks for reading!
