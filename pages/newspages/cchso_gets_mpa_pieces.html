Cypress Creek HS Orchestra gets new MPA Pieces

Lucas Villanueva - 1/15/25

Today the Cypress Creek Orchestra just got all of their new MPA pieces for the 2024-2025 season. This includes the orchestra first B Level piece, since prior they have been playing C Level pieces for years, however have been progressing to higher C level pieces.

So far from sight-reading alone, they sound good, and likely to complete these pieces with great performance, and fare easily on the MPA sight-reading grading.

Will the CCHS orchestra continue their straight superiors streak?
